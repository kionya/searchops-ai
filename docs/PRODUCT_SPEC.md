# PRODUCT_SPEC.md

## Product
SearchOps AI is a SaaS platform for SEO/AEO/GEO diagnosis, content planning, structured data generation, AI visibility monitoring, and work order automation.

## Primary Users
- SEO operators who need repeatable site audits and work orders.
- Content teams who need AEO/GEO-ready content plans.
- Compliance reviewers who need medical advertising risk flags before publication.
- Engineering teams who need structured, testable issue data instead of narrative-only reports.

## Phase 0 Scope
- Establish a TypeScript-first monorepo foundation.
- Create runnable app/package shells.
- Add placeholder tests and validation contracts.
- Keep product logic out until the core specs are approved.

## Non-goals
- No live external API integrations.
- No production crawler behavior.
- No LLM-dependent SEO issue detection.
- No medical content auto-publication.
